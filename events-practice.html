Shortcuts

You are going to paint the flag. Here are some tips:


press SHIFT + b and the upper color will change to BLUE

press CTRL + y and the lower color will change to YELLOW


press SHIFT + w and the upper color will change to WHITE

press CTRL + r and the lower color will change to RED


press SHIFT + r and the upper color will change to RED

press CTRL + b and the lower color will change to BLACK
